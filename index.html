Declaration of Intent
The Campaign

WHY I AM RUNNING

The Office of the Assistant General Secretary exists to support coordination, documentation, and continuity within the association — and I believe there is real room to improve how these are done.

Student organisations often struggle with scattered records, weak follow-up systems, and poor continuity between administrations. I am not here to criticise what has been done before — I am here to build on it, improve it, and put in place structures that will serve NIMechE‑UI long after my tenure ends.

I am already taking practical steps to implement systems that can make a lasting difference — not just during one tenure, but beyond it. I humbly seek your support and your votes.

MY CONSTITUTIONAL DUTIES

The following are the duties of the Assistant General Secretary as defined by the NIMechE‑UI SRC Constitution. These are not personal promises — they are institutional responsibilities I am fully committed to fulfilling.

01
Primary Duty
Assist the General Secretary in all secretariat duties and act in their absence — ensuring no gap in administrative operations at any time.
02
Succession Duty
Act as General Secretary during vacancy occasioned by resignation, suspension, impeachment, or incapacity — pending a by-election within 15 working days.
03
Secretariat Duty
Maintain the NIMechE‑UI secretariat in an orderly, functional, and professionally managed state throughout the entire session.
04
Database Duty
Compile, maintain, and regularly update the NIMechE‑UI member database across all levels — ensuring accessibility for authorised executive use.
05
Recording Duty
Take minutes at executive meetings in the absence of the General Secretary, and ensure all resolutions are properly documented and forwarded.
06
Delegated Duty
Perform other duties as delegated by the General Secretary — serving as a dependable and proactive member of the executive secretariat at all times.
Plans & Implementation
Six Plans

WHAT I AM DOING

Each plan is grounded in my constitutional duties and is already being worked on — not merely proposed. I believe in demonstrating commitment before asking for trust.

01
STRUCTURED DIGITAL ARCHIVING
Meeting records, circulars, executive reports, and association documents — properly organised, digitally archived, labelled, and permanently retrievable. Building on existing secretariat practice to add a reliable digital layer that ensures records are never lost between administrations.
In Progress
02
SECRETARIAT ORGANISATION & EFFICIENCY
Supporting the General Secretary in maintaining a well-coordinated administrative centre. Improving the structure of administrative records, ensuring orderliness within the secretariat, and making it a professional, reliable reference point for the entire executive council.
In Progress
03
TASK TRACKING & FOLLOW-UP COORDINATION
Ensuring that every decision made at executive meetings translates into a completed, tracked action item. Automated reminders, deadline visibility, and progress tracking — so nothing falls through the cracks and every assigned responsibility is followed up on.
✦ Live
04
MEMBER DATABASE MANAGEMENT
Fulfilling the constitutional mandate to compile, maintain, and update the NIMechE‑UI member database — from 100L to 500L. Structured for accuracy, ease of update, and straightforward handover to the next administration.
In Progress
05
CONTINUITY‑DRIVEN HANDOVER
The most important metric of any administration is what it leaves behind. Every system I build will be fully documented, portable, and designed for a clean handover — so the incoming AGS is fully operational within 30 minutes, not 30 days.
✦ Live
06
FRESHERS ONBOARDING & INTEGRATION
Collaborating with the Vice President to ensure a smooth transition and proper integration of incoming 100‑Level students into NIMechE‑UI. This includes structured orientation, proper sensitisation on NIMechE-UI benefits and structure, induction into the member database, and controlled access to the NIMechE‑UI Public Archives — specifically the 100L educational materials folder — via the Nextcloud portal.
Planned
The Live System
Already Built  ·  Already Running

THE LIVE SYSTEM

I have personally designed, configured, and deployed a self-hosted Nextcloud instance for NIMechE‑UI — running live on Microsoft Azure, accessible from any device at nimecheui.francecentral.cloudapp.azure.com. This is not a concept. It is operational today.

The portal provides structured team folders with role-based access control, 20 GB of educational archives for students, internal messaging via Talk, task and calendar management, and a 10-folder filing system purpose-built for NIMechE-UI — designed for complete handover in under 30 minutes.
